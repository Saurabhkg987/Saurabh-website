Research Highlights

Research Interests

Additive Manufacturing

Laser Powder Bed Fusion and Directed Energy Deposition techniques for fabricating biomedical and aerospace-grade titanium alloys.

Titanium Alloys

Development of Ti-6Al-4V and Ti–35Nb–5Ta–7Zr alloys for enhanced mechanical and biological performance in orthopedic applications.

Tribocorrosion

Investigating wear and corrosion synergy in physiological conditions for improved implant durability.

Microstructure & Crystallographic Texture

Using EBSD, TEM, and ECCI to study deformation mechanisms and texture evolution during processing.

Orthopedic Implants

High-temperature mechanical behavior studies to assess the long-term performance of titanium alloys in aerospace applications.

Plastic Deformation

Real-world validation of 3D-printed implants for upper-limb deformities through translational research and clinical application.
